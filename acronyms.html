💰 Commercial & Performance 11
CPL
Cost Per Lead
How much it costs to generate one lead. Core metric when pricing and evaluating campaigns.
EPL
Earnings Per Lead
Revenue earned for each lead delivered to a client. Used alongside CPL to calculate campaign margin.
CPA
Cost Per Acquisition
Total cost to acquire one paying customer, including all marketing and operational costs.
CPM
Cost Per Mille
Cost per 1,000 impressions. Used in broadcast and display advertising.
CTR
Click-Through Rate
Percentage of people who click a link after seeing it. Measures effectiveness of SMS and digital campaigns.
ROI
Return on Investment
How much revenue was generated relative to cost. The ultimate campaign success measure.
KPI
Key Performance Indicator
A measurable target used to track performance. Every campaign, team and project has KPIs.
SLA
Service Level Agreement
A formal contract defining commitments on volumes, quality and timelines. 3Way has SLAs with major clients like FNB.
EPS
Earnings Per Sale
Revenue earned when a lead converts into a confirmed sale. Used to measure the downstream value of lead quality.
EPQ
Earnings Per Quote
Revenue earned when a quote is produced from a lead. Common in insurance campaigns where quoting is a milestone before sale.
PPC
Pay Per Click
A paid advertising model where advertisers are charged each time a user clicks an advert. Used in Google and social media campaigns.
📡 Channels & Technology 11
AVM
Automated Voice Message
Pre-recorded outbound voice calls sent at scale. A core 3Way channel — runs through the Archimedes (Archi) platform.
SMS
Short Message Service
Text messaging channel. Used for outbound lead generation campaigns and customer communications.
USSD
Unstructured Supplementary Service Data
The "dial *120*xxx#" technology used on mobile phones. Common in SA for low-data customer journeys.
DCB
Direct Carrier Billing
Payment charged directly to a customer's mobile phone bill. SayHi originally operated on DCB rails.
VAS
Value Added Service
Mobile services billed to a subscriber's account beyond standard calls and data. SayHi operates as a VAS entity.
API
Application Programming Interface
A technical connection between two software systems. Used to exchange lead data, access scorecards and connect platforms.
PCM
Please Call Me
A free mobile network service that allows a consumer to send a callback request to another number. 3Way runs campaigns on PCM channels across operators like Vodacom.
CLI
Calling Line Identification
The phone number displayed on an inbound or outbound call. Used in call centre operations and campaign tracking.
OTP
One-Time Password
A single-use code sent via SMS or voice to verify identity. Used in customer authentication flows.
MNO
Mobile Network Operator
A mobile carrier — e.g. MTN, Vodacom, Telkom. 3Way runs campaigns across multiple MNOs.
MSISDN
Mobile Station Integrated Services Digital Network Number
The full international format of a mobile phone number. Used in data files and telco systems.
🔒 Data & Compliance 7
POPIA
Protection of Personal Information Act
South Africa's data privacy law. Governs how 3Way can collect, store and use customer data. Consent and opt-out management are critical obligations.
DW
Data Warehouse
3Way's major infrastructure project. A centralised system for storing and querying all business data, being built with Elucidate.
ETL
Extract, Transform, Load
The process of pulling data from source systems, cleaning and formatting it, then loading it into a warehouse or database.
POC
Proof of Concept
A small-scale test to validate an idea before full rollout. 3Way runs POCs with partners before committing to full campaigns.
CRM
Customer Relationship Management
Software used to track customer interactions, leads and relationships. Referenced in campaign management and lead routing.
PII
Personally Identifiable Information
Any data that can identify an individual — name, ID number, phone number. Subject to POPIA protections.
CIPC
Companies and Intellectual Property Commission
The South African body that registers companies. 3Way's annual returns are filed here.
🏦 Financial Services 9
FA
Financial Advisor
A licensed professional who sells financial products (insurance, investments). 3Way generates leads for FA call centres.
FSC
Financial Services Conduct
Shorthand for FSCA compliance requirements. All financial product leads must meet FSCA conduct standards.
FSCA
Financial Sector Conduct Authority
The South African regulator for financial services. Governs how insurance, credit and investment products are marketed.
NCA
National Credit Act
Legislation governing credit products in South Africa. Relevant to debt counselling (FindBetter) and credit lead generation.
NCR
National Credit Regulator
The body that enforces the NCA. FindBetter and debt counselling partners must be NCR-registered.
FNB
First National Bank
3Way's largest lead generation client. A formal SLA is in place with primary contacts Thato Modise and Kugen Pillay.
DC
Debt Counselling / Debt Counsellor
A regulated service helping over-indebted consumers restructure their debt. FindBetter operates in this space.
JV
Joint Venture
A business partnership between two or more companies. FindBetter is a JV between 3Way and Confluent.
FSP
Financial Service Provider
A regulated entity authorised to sell financial products such as insurance, credit or investment services. Clients who buy leads from 3Way are typically FSPs.
🛠️ Internal Platforms & Tools 6
Archi
Archimedes
3Way's AVM delivery platform operated by BizVoIP. Handles outbound call campaigns at scale.
DLVR
DLVR Platform
Internal delivery and campaign platform. Used alongside Archi and Dripcell for managing outbound messaging campaigns.
SALT
SALT Platform
Reporting and volume tracking system. Daily SALT volume reports are a key operational indicator.
Dripcell
Dripcell
Software platform used in 3Way's campaign operations. Part of the core tech stack.
MNM
MNM Channel
A key internal distribution channel encompassing PCM, OnePin and SimPush. MNM Channel Relaunch is a current high-priority project.
SimPush
SIM Push
A channel that delivers messages directly to a subscriber's SIM card. Part of the MNM channel suite.
✉️ Communication & Email 6
NB
Nota Bene
Latin for "note well." Used in email subject lines and messages to flag something important that requires attention.
FYI
For Your Information
Information shared for awareness only. No action is required from the recipient.
CC
Carbon Copy
Email function used to copy additional recipients for visibility. Everyone on the email can see who was CC'd.
BCC
Blind Carbon Copy
Email copy where additional recipients are hidden from the main recipients. Used when visibility of recipients needs to be restricted.
EOM
End of Message
Used at the end of an email subject line to indicate the full message is in the subject — no body text required.
EOD
End of Day
A common deadline marker. "Please send by EOD" means by close of business.
⚙️ Operations & Quality 2
QA
Quality Assurance
Function responsible for monitoring calls, reviewing lead quality and ensuring compliance with client and regulatory standards.
BI
Business Intelligence
Function responsible for analysing performance data and producing reports. BI outputs drive decisions on campaign spend, channel mix and lead quality.
📖 Glossary — Key Terms 14
Lead
Lead
An individual who shows interest in a product by responding to marketing activity. A contact record capturing their details and intent for a call centre to follow up.
Lead Gen
Lead Generation
The process of capturing details of potential customers to create sales opportunities. This is 3Way's core business.
Affiliates
Affiliates
Third-party companies or individuals that generate leads on behalf of the business, typically paid on a CPL or revenue share basis.
Profiled Data
Profiled Data
Data that has been segmented using demographic or behavioural attributes such as age, income, gender or geography to improve targeting.
Opt-in DB
Opt-in Database
A database of individuals who have given consent to receive marketing communications. Required for POPIA-compliant campaigns.
White Label
White Label / Unbranded
Advertising or landing pages without a specific brand attached. Used when the advertiser does not want their brand visible at the point of capture.
Volumes
Volumes
The number of leads required or delivered within a specific timeframe. Volumes are the primary commercial metric in client SLAs.
Conversions
Conversions
The percentage of leads that convert into sales. A key measure of lead quality and call centre effectiveness.
Channels
Channels
The marketing mediums used to generate leads. 3Way operates across SMS, AVM, PCM, USSD, SimPush, OnePin, Google PPC, web affiliates, radio and TV.
Airtime Vouchers
Airtime Voucher Campaigns
Lead generation promotions tied to airtime incentives. Customers enter their details to receive a free airtime voucher, generating a lead in the process.
Street Pole Ads
Street Pole Advertising
Outdoor advertising on street poles directing prospects to respond to an offer via a phone number, USSD code or landing page.
Web Affiliates
Web & Web Affiliates
Banner adverts placed on partner websites that drive traffic to lead capture forms. Paid on a CPL or revenue share basis.
Surveys
Survey-based Lead Gen
Lead generation through survey platforms where a user completes a questionnaire and their details are captured as a lead.
Profits
Profit
Revenue minus cost. In lead generation, this is typically EPL minus CPL multiplied by volume delivered.
