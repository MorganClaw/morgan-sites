📖 Acronym Dictionary

Every acronym you'll hear at 3Way. Search below or browse by category.

🔍
💰 Commercial & Performance 8
CPL
Cost Per Lead
How much it costs to generate one lead. Core metric used when pricing and evaluating campaigns.
EPL
Earnings Per Lead
Revenue earned for each lead delivered to a client. Used alongside CPL to calculate campaign margin.
CPA
Cost Per Acquisition
Total cost to acquire one paying customer, including all marketing and operational costs.
CPM
Cost Per Mille
Cost per 1,000 impressions. Used in broadcast and display advertising.
CTR
Click-Through Rate
Percentage of people who click a link after seeing it. Used to measure the effectiveness of SMS and digital campaigns.
ROI
Return on Investment
How much revenue was generated relative to the cost. The ultimate campaign success measure.
KPI
Key Performance Indicator
A measurable target used to track performance. Every campaign, team and project has KPIs.
SLA
Service Level Agreement
A formal contract defining commitments on volumes, quality and timelines. 3Way has SLAs with major clients like FNB.
📡 Channels & Technology 11
AVM
Automated Voice Message
Pre-recorded outbound voice calls sent at scale. A core 3Way channel — runs through Archimedes (Archi) platform.
SMS
Short Message Service
Text messaging channel. Used for outbound lead generation campaigns and customer communications.
USSD
Unstructured Supplementary Service Data
The "dial *120*xxx#" technology used on mobile phones. Common in SA for low-data customer journeys.
DCB
Direct Carrier Billing
Payment method where charges go directly to a customer's mobile phone bill. SayHi originally operated on DCB rails before pivoting to credit card.
VAS
Value Added Service
Mobile services billed to a subscriber's account beyond standard calls/data. SayHi operates as a VAS entity.
API
Application Programming Interface
A technical connection between two software systems. Used to exchange lead data, access scorecards and connect platforms.
PCM
Pre-Connection Message
A channel where subscribers hear a short audio message before their call connects. Vodacom PCM campaigns are managed by 3Way.
CLI
Calling Line Identification
The phone number displayed on an inbound or outbound call. Used in call centre operations and campaign tracking.
OTP
One-Time Password
A single-use code sent via SMS or voice to verify identity. Used in customer authentication flows.
MNO
Mobile Network Operator
A mobile carrier — e.g. MTN, Vodacom, Telkom. 3Way runs campaigns across multiple MNOs.
MSISDN
Mobile Station Integrated Services Digital Network Number
The full international format of a mobile phone number. Used in data files and telco systems instead of plain phone numbers.
🔒 Data & Compliance 7
POPIA
Protection of Personal Information Act
South Africa's data privacy law. Governs how 3Way can collect, store and use customer data. Consent and opt-out management are critical obligations.
DW
Data Warehouse
3Way's major infrastructure project. A centralised system for storing and querying all business data. Being built with Elucidate.
ETL
Extract, Transform, Load
The process of pulling data from source systems, cleaning/formatting it and loading it into a warehouse or database.
POC
Proof of Concept
A small-scale test to validate an idea before full rollout. 3Way runs POCs with partners like FindBetter, Tracker and Matagen before committing.
CRM
Customer Relationship Management
Software used to track customer interactions, leads and relationships. Referenced in campaign management and lead routing.
PII
Personally Identifiable Information
Any data that can identify an individual — name, ID number, phone number. Subject to POPIA protections.
CIPC
Companies and Intellectual Property Commission
The South African body that registers companies. 3Way's annual returns are filed here. Due date: 27 March.
🏦 Financial Services 8
FA
Financial Advisor
A licensed professional who sells financial products (insurance, investments). 3Way generates leads for FA call centres.
FSC
Financial Services Conduct
Shorthand for FSCA compliance requirements. All financial product leads must meet FSCA conduct standards.
FSCA
Financial Sector Conduct Authority
The South African regulator for financial services. Governs how insurance, credit and investment products are marketed.
NCA
National Credit Act
Legislation governing credit products in South Africa. Relevant to debt counselling (FindBetter) and credit lead generation.
NCR
National Credit Regulator
The body that enforces the NCA. FindBetter and debt counselling partners must be NCR-registered.
FNB
First National Bank
3Way's largest lead generation client. A formal SLA is in place. Primary contacts: Thato Modise and Kugen Pillay.
DC
Debt Counselling / Debt Counsellor
A regulated service helping over-indebted consumers restructure their debt. FindBetter operates in this space.
JV
Joint Venture
A business partnership between two or more companies. FindBetter is a JV between 3Way and Confluent.
🛠️ Internal Platforms & Tools 6
Archi
Archimedes
3Way's AVM (automated voice message) delivery platform. Operated by BizVoIP/Frans Schneider. Handles outbound call campaigns at scale.
DLVR
DLVR Platform
Internal delivery/campaign platform. Used alongside Archi and Dripcell for managing outbound messaging campaigns.
SALT
SALT Platform
Reporting/volume tracking system. Daily SALT volume reports arrive in the inbox and are a key operational indicator.
Dripcell
Dripcell
Software platform used in 3Way's campaign operations. Part of the core tech stack referenced in platform standing items.
MNM
MNM Channel
A key internal distribution channel encompassing PCM, OnePin and SimPush. MNM Channel Relaunch is a current high-priority project.
SimPush
SIM Push
A channel that delivers messages directly to a subscriber's SIM card. Part of the MNM channel suite. Approval contact: Zunaid.
CEO
Chief Executive Officer
Dylan Koen is CEO of 3Way Group. Leads overall business strategy and operations.
COO
Chief Operating Officer
Yoav Dogan is COO of 3Way Group. David is COO of FindBetter.
CMO
Chief Marketing Officer
Victor — recently discussed as a potential CMO appointment at 3Way Group.
EXCO
Executive Committee
The senior leadership team of 3Way Group. Ricky is a member and shareholder.
B2B
Business to Business
Selling to companies rather than individual consumers. Some 3Way partnerships and data services are B2B.
B2C
Business to Consumer
Selling directly to individual consumers. Lead generation campaigns (FNB, Vodacom) are B2C focused.
