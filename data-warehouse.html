Strategic Value — 7 Pillars

The Strategic Pillars

Seven measurable outcomes that define the warehouse's value to the business. Each pillar has a clear target and timeline.

01
Reduce Marketing Cost
+

Stop spending on contacts that will never respond. Suppress dead numbers, deduplicate across entities, and target only qualified leads.

15–25% reduction in CPQL, Year 1
02
Save Resource Time
+

Eliminate manual data wrangling, automate reporting, and free technical teams for higher-value work. From 2-day DSAR resolution to 4 hours.

60%+ reduction in manual wrangling, 6 months
03
Right Customer. Right Time. Right Channel.
+

Move beyond contact rate to precision. Know which consumer is ready, on which channel, for which product — and reach them at the moment they are most likely to respond.

10–15% RPC improvement, Year 1
04
Improve Customer Quality
+

Higher quality leads mean better conversion, lower churn and stronger client relationships. Use data to distinguish a likely buyer from a number that will never convert — before the call is made.

Measurable uplift in lead-to-sale conversion
05
Better Customer Experience
+

Consumers who receive relevant offers at the right moment have a fundamentally different experience. Suppress irrelevant contacts, personalise product matching, and protect 3Way's reputation with end consumers.

Reduction in consumer complaints and opt-outs
06
Enable Scoring and Modelling
+

Propensity to respond, convert, pay. Build predictive models that transform campaign selection from guesswork to precision targeting.

2–4x campaign lift vs unscored baseline
07
Monetisable Data Asset
+

Data as a product. Scored segments at premium pricing. A data asset that makes the business acquirable and the valuation defensible.

Data as a revenue line

Scoring, Modelling & AI

The data warehouse is not just a storage layer. It is the foundation for a scoring and modelling capability that will fundamentally change how 3Way targets, qualifies and converts. This is a core reason the warehouse is being built.

🎯

Why Scoring Changes Everything

Today, marketing spend is allocated across a broad contact base with limited ability to predict who will respond, convert or qualify. With a unified warehouse, every record can be scored before a rand is spent. Propensity to respond. Propensity to convert. Creditworthiness signals. Contactability likelihood. Campaigns shift from volume-based to precision-based — and cost per qualified lead drops accordingly.

Propensity Modelling
Credit Scoring
Contactability Scoring
🤝

Matogen — Scorecard Partner

Matogen is 3Way's third-party scoring partner. Their phone scorecard, built on 3Way's own interaction data, predicts which leads are most likely to pass MTN's Illula system before a marketing rand is spent on them. The warehouse provides the clean, unified data layer that makes this scoring reliable and scalable across all entities.

Phone Scorecard

Internal AI & Automation

Beyond third-party scorecards, the warehouse enables 3Way to build and run its own models using internal resources. Automated campaign audience selection. Dynamic suppression lists. Real-time scoring at the point of lead acquisition. The intelligence layer that turns data into decisions without manual intervention.
