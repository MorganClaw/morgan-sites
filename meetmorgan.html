The loop that
never stops.

Most AI tools start from scratch every session. An AI agent runs continuously — context persists, tasks stay live, every interaction builds on the last. And every person at 3Way can have one.

01

A message, a voice note, a forwarded email. Any format. Your agent receives the instruction and confirms understanding immediately.
