PCM (Standard)

Please Call Me (Integrated)

PCMi

Same PCM trigger but the customer can request a callback directly from the USSD ad screen. No extra dial needed — lower friction, higher conversion.

SIM Push / OnePin

SimPush & OnePin

Offer pushed directly to the customer's SIM. Two-button response: "Call Me" or "Not Interested". Call Me generates a live lead instantly.
